Our main project

Our focus project

Our current main project is running Wangan Midnight Maximum Tune 6 RR series servers on computers. We're pleased to accept coders that can contribute to our project. If your a coder and want to participate, feel free to send us an email to join.

Wangan Midnight Maximum Tune is an arcade racing video game developed and published by NAMCO and based on the Wangan Midnight manga. It is the first entry of the Wangan Midnight Maximum Tune series and the second entry to the Wangan Midnight arcade series after Wangan Midnight R
